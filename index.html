About Me

The reason I enjoy web development more than just a career choice, but as a hobby, is because it allows me to use both sides of my brain. What web development has allowed me to do is consider problems or tasks with analysis and then accomplish them with creativity. To expand my knowledge on this subject in hope to pursue a career, I completed a full stack web development bootcamp at the University of Texas at Austin. I also have freelance experience building and maintaining a UI for a local food trailer. I strive to be continuously learning along as an excellent communicator and collaborator, who opens all their projects to feedback and critique, because I know ultimately that it will allow me to become a better developer.

I work excellent with both front end/back end development tools such as:

  • HTML/CSS
  • Javascript/Jquery/DOM
  • Node.JS
  • API calls with JSON/AJAX
  • Firebase & Databases
